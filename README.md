# E-Commerce-Customer-Intelligence-Dashboard
End-to-end e-commerce analytics project featuring customer segmentation, spending driver analysis, funnel analytics, cart abandonment insights, and premium subscription intelligence for business growth. Built with HTML, JavaScript, Chart.js, and AI.


A business analytics case study exploring the behavior of 1 million synthetic e-commerce shoppers through customer segmentation, revenue driver analysis, cart abandonment intelligence, conversion funnel analytics, and premium subscription insights.

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to understand customer behavior and identify opportunities for business growth.

The dashboard was designed to answer key business questions:

* Who generates the most revenue?
* What factors drive customer spending?
* Why do customers abandon their carts?
* Which customer segments deserve marketing investment?
* Who is most likely to subscribe to premium services?

## Dataset

Dataset: E-Commerce Shopper Behavior & Lifestyle Dataset

Characteristics:

* 1,000,000 synthetic users
* 57 features
* Demographics
* Lifestyle and health factors
* Shopping behavior
* Platform engagement metrics

The dataset is fully synthetic and intended for educational and analytical purposes.

## Analytics Framework

### 1. Customer Segmentation

Customers are grouped into behavioral segments:

* Champions
* Loyalists
* Price Hunters
* Browsers
* At-Risk Customers

Business objective:

Identify high-value customers and prioritize retention efforts.

### 2. Revenue Driver Analysis

Key drivers of customer spending are analyzed using correlation and behavioral metrics.

Examples:

* Income level
* Brand loyalty
* App usage frequency
* Social media influence

Business objective:

Understand which factors have the strongest relationship with monthly spending.

### 3. Cart Abandonment Intelligence

Analyze factors associated with higher abandonment rates.

Examples:

* Financial stress
* Device type
* Payment methods
* Shopping behavior

Business objective:

Reduce lost revenue and improve checkout completion.

### 4. Conversion Funnel Analysis

Funnel stages:

1. Product Views
2. Wishlist
3. Cart
4. Purchase

Business objective:

Identify where customers drop off and improve conversion performance.

### 5. Premium Subscription Intelligence

Identify users most likely to subscribe to premium services based on behavioral signals.

Business objective:

Support targeted premium acquisition campaigns.

## Key Business Insights

Examples:

* Premium subscribers spend significantly more than non-premium users.
* Financial stress is associated with higher cart abandonment.
* High-income customers contribute disproportionately to revenue.
* Customer loyalty is strongly associated with spending behavior.
